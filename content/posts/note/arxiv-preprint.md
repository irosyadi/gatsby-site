- [Arxiv Vanity](https://www.arxiv-vanity.com/) convert arxiv-pdf to html

- [Arxiv Times](https://arxivtimes.herokuapp.com/) ML news from Arxiv
- [Daily Arxiv](https://dailyarxiv.com/) check arxiv articles based on date
